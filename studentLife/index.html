Student Life

What is it Like for a Student at d.tech?

At d.tech, students have a different experience than at a traditional school.
Here are some programs and activities that only d.tech has

@d.tech

Students are all assigned and put into an @d.tech advisory. The students will meet with their advisory group every day in the morning to discuss anything that is going on in their lives and also compete in activities with other advisories. Advisory groups stay together for the rest of their time at Design Tech and bond together during their time together.

Intersession

Instead of have one elective that is taken throughout the year daily, students have intersession to pursue other interests. Intersession is a two week period that occurs four times each school year. During these two weeks, students may choose a morning and afternoon class that is offered to explore outside of the usual school subjects.

Community Meetings

Community Meetings are times when the school meets together in order to find out what's happening in the community and come together. Administration starts it off with the announcements, followed by some community activities planned out by leadership clubs.
